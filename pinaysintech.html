Pinays in Tech

With our new partnership with FilipinasInComputing (FilipinasIC) via Anita Borg Institute (the annual organizer of Grace Hopper Conference), we are looking to start new programs for our organization.

We are creating programs for engineering/technical women in FASTER as well as other programs broadly for Pinays in tech (design, product management, business/marketing/sales + other non-technical roles etc.)

We are organizing an event for March (International Women's Month) and want your feedback on what you'd like to see happen.

Please fill out this interest survey for if you're interested in getting involved, supporting, or sponsoring for end of March 2019. Thanks!
